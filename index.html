Movie List

  1. 1. Interstellar

    Its a great movie that reminds us how small we are as humanity. Its humbling to know that we dont know whats up there beyond the skies.

    The twist will keep you up all night long, its a masterclass in keeping you on your toes and your mind racing.

  3. 3. The Prestige

    You follow that I am an avid twist suspenseful thriller watcher and this solidifies that by being a great movie with a jaw dropping twist.

  4. 4. Coraline

    Its more than a cartoon its horror food for thought, its very complex and has many complexities to it. Think its just a cartoon? watch it... youll thank me.

  5. 5. The Ritual

    A great horror movie that tests our morals in friendship.

  6. 6. The Descent

    A good comedy movie with a powerful message.

  9. 9. The Truman Show

    What would you do if your life was always being watched?

  10. 10. Scary Movie

    No reason other than being insanely hilarious.
